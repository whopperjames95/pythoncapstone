Personal Aspiration APP:

This website/app will create the ability to sign up, and create an account. Also have the ability
to log in and out. Also it being a personal aspiration app, this app allows you to keep track of
personal goals, places you'd like to travel to, and also create your own bucket list.



3 Main Features of this App:

1. Create A goal list, you can add the goal, once completed you can delete the goal.
2. Create A traveling list, places you'd like to travel, will have ability to upload images to list.
3. Create a bucket list, will have ability to upload images as well to this list.


Other Features:
Will have a database, hence the reason for the sign up and creating an account ability.
